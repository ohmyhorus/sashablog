Who I am?


I'm a boy from Milan, my passions vary from classical to heavy music,
from Canova marbles to contemporary tacky (which I love with all my heart, this is my guilty pleasure)
from novels and fanfiction, to psychology (Jung), philosophy (I share the thick mustache with Nietzche),
sociology (Bauman) and weirdo stuff (guilty pleasure n 2) like Jodorowsky and Castaneda.
By pure chance and advice from one of my relatives, I began to get curious about front-ends and here I am,
to create a site.

Ah, true, I forgot to mention that sometimes I do something like "model" for my friend.
From classic tacky stuff, to more elaborate things of my and my friend mind.
Sometimes give birth to truly bizarre ideas and scenarios.
It all started from the fact that she didn't know who to take the pictures of, she was bored photographing food,
still life or landscapes, becouse Instagram was full of these photos back in 2010-11, and me offered like a volunteer,
I know she doesn't like to publish photos, because she does it for herself, but I decided, hanging out,
a bit also out of boredom and why not, to experiment with "creation", to give birth to a blog/site, where I collect all the photos taken,
as a tribute to his work done practically for free (where do you see such a thing nowadays?).

Let the journey begin...
