Who I am?

Let the journey begin...

Sulfur


Alchemical element = sulfur, atmospheric black metal. In terms of human body, it represented the soul.
In terms of the tria prima, sulfur was seen as the middling element connecting salt (high) and mercury (low).

Vapor-air



Alchemical element = air,gas state, vaporwave music,bright colors, blu.

Copper




Copper, rustic, shabby, grunge music, corrosion, copper or dark red color.

Sun



Gold, sun, heat.
In alchemic and Hermetic traditions, suns are used to symbolize a variety of concepts,
much like the sun in astrology. Suns can correspond to gold, citrinitas, generative masculine principles, imagery of "the king",
or Apollo, the fiery spirit or sulfur,the divine spark in man,nobility, or incorruptibility. Recurring images of specific solar motifs
can be found in the form of a "Dark" or "Black Sun", or a green lion devouring a sun (wikipedia).
